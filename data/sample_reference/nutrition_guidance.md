# Nutrition During Pregnancy

## General Principles
- Eat one extra meal daily during pregnancy
- Consume a variety of foods including fortified food items
- Use only adequately iodized/double fortified salt
- Take at least 2 hours rest during day and 8 hours at night

## Key Nutrient Sources

### Iron
- Green leafy vegetables (spinach, fenugreek, amaranth)
- Whole grains, cereals
- Dry fruits, nuts
- Meat (for non-vegetarians)
- Jaggery, dates, beetroot

### Calcium
- Milk, curd, paneer, buttermilk
- Sesame seeds, almonds
- Soya milk
- Eggs

### Proteins
- Dal, pulses (at least 2 cups daily for vegetarians)
- Paneer, milk products
- Nuts (45 grams daily)
- Eggs, meat, chicken, fish (non-vegetarian)
- Soya beans, combined grains

### Vitamins
- Orange and dark green vegetables
- Citrus fruits, apple, tomato, amla
- Sunlight exposure for Vitamin D
- Milk and milk products

## Trimester-Specific Guidance

### 1st Trimester
- Folic acid 5mg daily (critical for neural tube development)
- IFA tablet from 4th month
- Focus on nausea management with small frequent meals
- Avoid raw papaya, excessive caffeine

### 2nd Trimester
- Additional 300 calories daily
- Increased protein (65g daily)
- Calcium 1200mg daily (2 tablets)
- Albendazole single dose
- Iron-rich foods emphasis

### 3rd Trimester
- Additional 500 calories daily
- Protein target 75g daily
- Continue calcium and IFA
- Frequent small meals
- Monitor weight gain closely

## Special Conditions

### Anemia (Hb < 10 g/dL)
- Double IFA tablets (morning and evening)
- Iron-rich foods at every meal
- Vitamin C with iron for absorption
- AVOID tea/coffee with meals
- Referral if Hb < 7

### Gestational Diabetes
- Low glycemic index foods
- Whole grains, oats, brown rice
- High fiber vegetables
- Small frequent meals (6 per day)
- AVOID refined sugar, white rice excess

### Underweight
- Extra calorie-dense foods
- Ghee/oil supplementation
- THR (Take Home Ration) from Anganwadi
- Nuts and dry fruits daily
- Supplementary nutrition from ICDS

## POSHAN 2.0 / Saksham Entitlements
- Supplementary nutrition from Anganwadi Centre
- Take Home Ration (THR) for pregnant women
- Hot cooked meal at AWC
- Nutrition counseling at every ANC visit
- Growth monitoring and promotion
