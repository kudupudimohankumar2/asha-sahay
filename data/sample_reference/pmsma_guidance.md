# Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA)

## About the Programme
PMSMA provides free antenatal checkup by specialist/doctor on the 9th of every month for pregnant women in their 2nd or 3rd trimester.

## Key Features
- Fixed day: 9th of every month
- Available at government health facilities
- Specialist/doctor examination
- Comprehensive antenatal checkup
- High-risk pregnancy identification and management
- Free of cost

## Services Provided on PMSMA Day
1. Full antenatal checkup by doctor
2. Ultrasonography (if not done previously)
3. High-risk screening
4. Specialist consultation for complicated cases
5. Blood tests: Hemoglobin, blood sugar, urine analysis
6. Blood pressure monitoring
7. Weight and abdominal examination

## Who Should Attend
- All pregnant women in 2nd trimester (14-27 weeks)
- All pregnant women in 3rd trimester (28-40 weeks)
- Priority for high-risk cases
- Previously identified complications

## ASHA Worker Role
- Identify and mobilize pregnant women for 9th of month visits
- Ensure women in 2nd/3rd trimester attend at least one PMSMA checkup
- Accompany high-risk women to the facility
- Follow up on recommendations from PMSMA visit
- Update records with PMSMA visit findings

## Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY)
- Cash assistance for institutional delivery
- Available at public health facilities and accredited private hospitals

## Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)
- Cash benefit for first live child in the family
- 1st installment: After early registration at AWC/health facility
- 2nd installment: After at least one ANC (claimable after 6 months pregnancy)
- 3rd installment: After child birth registration and first immunization cycle

## Janani-Shishu Suraksha Karyakram (JSSK)
For pregnant women:
- Free and cashless delivery
- Free caesarean section
- Free drugs and consumables
- Free diagnostics (blood, urine, ultrasonography)
- Free diet during stay
- Free provision of blood
- Exemption from user charges
- Free transport (home to facility, between facilities, and back)
