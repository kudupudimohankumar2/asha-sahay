# Antenatal Care (ANC) Guidelines

## Registration
Register at the nearest health facility as soon as pregnancy is detected, ideally within the first trimester.

## ANC Visit Schedule
- **ANC-1**: As soon as period is missed or within first 3 months. Registration, blood group, Rh typing, hemoglobin, urine tests, HIV screening, syphilis test, BP, weight, TT-1.
- **ANC-2**: 4th-6th month. Hemoglobin, BP, weight, urine tests, TT-2, ultrasonography, fetal heart rate.
- **ANC-3**: 7th-8th month. Hemoglobin, BP, weight, urine tests, GDM screening, fetal heart rate, abdominal examination.
- **ANC-4**: 9th month. Hemoglobin, BP, weight, urine tests, fetal heart rate, fetal position, delivery plan.

## Essential Checks at Every Visit
- Blood pressure measurement
- Hemoglobin estimation
- Urine albumin and sugar testing
- Weight measurement (target gain: 9-11 kg total, at least 1 kg/month in last 6 months)
- Abdominal examination

## Iron Folic Acid (IFA) Supplementation
- Take 1 IFA tablet daily starting from 4th month
- Minimum 180 tablets during pregnancy
- If anemic: 2 IFA tablets daily (morning and evening)
- Take with Vitamin C for better absorption
- Avoid tea/coffee near meal times

## Calcium Supplementation
- Take 2 calcium tablets daily for at least 6 months after 1st trimester

## Tetanus Toxoid
- TT-1: When pregnancy is confirmed
- TT-2: After 1 month of TT-1
- Single booster if previously vaccinated within 3 years

## Albendazole
- Single dose of 400mg after 1st trimester

## Weight Gain Guidelines
- Total recommended gain: 9-11 kg during pregnancy
- At least 1 kg per month during last 6 months
- Monitor at every ANC visit
